DANDOBA HILLS

The Dandoba Hill Forest Preserve is about a 25-minute drive from Sangli. This woodland preserve is home to a variety of flora and fauna, as well as a few historically noteworthy ancient structures. This preserve’s hills are a popular destination for trekking and lengthy excursions. Dandoba Hill Station also hosts an annual mini-marathon race, which is a popular event.



PANHALA FORT

The Panhala fort is a tiny hill fort near Kolhapur, Maharashtra. In the 16th century, the battle of Pavankhind took place here. This is an excellent location for those who enjoy visiting historical sites. Panhala also served some trekking spots perfect for beginners. It is also absolutely worth seeing because of its historical significance. It is one of the best places for trekking near Kolhapur.



AMBOLI WATERFALL

Amboli is a lovely hill station in the southern range of the Sahyadri hills, 690 meters above sea level, right before the plain coastal beaches of Goa. Amboli is encircled on three sides by the Sahyadri hill valleys, with the Konkan seashore on the fourth. Amboli’s climate, flora, and fauna make for a wonderful mix for travelers. In addition, the clean and calm hills here provide rich hot biodiversity to enjoy while sightseeing. It is one of the best adventure places near Kolhapur if you like to do hikes and treks near a waterfall.
